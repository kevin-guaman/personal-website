About me

Curious by nature, consistent by habit

My love of learning started early. An elementary school teacher once told me that "knowledge is power," and that idea has stuck with me ever since — it's why I ask questions, dig into new topics, and look for ways to keep improving. Discovering the internet opened that world even further, showing me the creativity and accomplishments of people everywhere and pushing me to build my own interests. It taught me a simple but powerful lesson: consistent effort, applied over time, can help anyone reach goals that once felt out of reach.

Learning artificial intelligence has been one of the most transformative parts of my journey so far. It's changed how I approach problems, helping me complete projects more efficiently, work with greater confidence, and take on challenges I might have once hesitated to try. I've put that mindset into practice as a Fellow in the MESA Alumni Lab program, and I'm currently continuing to grow my skills as a participant in the Pursuit AI Native program. Every program, project, and problem I work through adds another layer to the developer I'm becoming — someone ready to learn fast, adapt, and contribute from day one.
